About

I graduated high school a year early and left home at 17 to start touring as a bassist, moved to Los Angeles shortly thereafter, and started to pursue a life of music. I have been playing music professionally ever since while also doing graphic design work on the side.

For the past few years, I have been dreaming about a change in career to something more stable and reliable than my life in music. I have always loved math, science, problem solving, and logical thinking, so I decided to enroll in a bootcamp and learn how to code, and it turns out, I really love it!

In 2023, I graduated from Thinkful’s Bootcamp where I studied a plethora of full-stack skills including things like Javascript, node, express, and react. I am also teaching myself the basics of Python and PHP in my free time. I have an incredibly strong drive to learn and grow, and I am really looking forward to what the future holds for me as a web developer / software engineer!
